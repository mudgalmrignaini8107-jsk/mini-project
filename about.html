Our Mission & The SIH PS 2025 Challenge

The **City Care Portal** was developed as a direct solution to a critical problem statement (PS) identified in the **Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025**: the urgent need for a transparent, efficient, and data-driven system for citizens to report and track civic issues.

The Problem Statement

The traditional grievance system often suffers from delayed response times, lack of geo-spatial data, and poor feedback loops. This leads to citizen frustration and unresolved municipal issues like persistent potholes, non-functional streetlights, and delayed garbage collection.

Our Solution: A 3-Pillar Approach

We solve this by focusing on three key technology pillars:

  • Crowdsourced Reporting: Any registered citizen can instantly report an issue using their mobile device.
  • Intelligent Validation: Automated processing using **Image Analysis (AI)** validates the existence and severity of the issue, eliminating fake reports.
  • Real-Time Geo-tagging: Every report is automatically tagged with GPS coordinates, providing municipal teams with the precise location for immediate action.

Our commitment is to bridge the gap between citizens and local governance, driving faster issue resolution and fostering a truly smart city environment.
