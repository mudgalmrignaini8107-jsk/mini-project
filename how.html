The 5-Step Resolution Cycle

Our platform simplifies the process of reporting and resolving civic issues by leveraging citizen input and smart technology. Here is how your report drives real change:

  1. Report & Capture Evidence:

    A citizen submits an issue with location-tagged photos and a brief description.

  2. AI Validation & Geo-Pinning:

    The system analyzes the image, classifies the issue, and verifies GPS coordinates.

  3. Department Assignment & Tracking:

    The municipal team receives the report, and the citizen gets a tracking ID.

  4. Resolution & Action:

    The field team fixes the issue and uploads a resolution photo.

  5. Citizen Feedback Loop:

    The citizen confirms whether the fix is satisfactory.

By streamlining this cycle, we aim to reduce the average resolution time by **over 40%**.
