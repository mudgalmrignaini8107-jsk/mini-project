Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about reporting issues, tracking progress, and the technology behind the City Care Portal.


Q: How does the AI Image Analysis work?
The system uses a Machine Learning model trained on municipal data to detect and classify civic issues, and measure severity such as pothole depth or width.
Q: What happens if I submit a false report?
AI validation and geo-tagging reduce false reports. Reports flagged as fraudulent by officials may lead to temporary account restrictions.
Q: Can I track the status of my report?
Yes. You receive a tracking ID after submission. You can check status like Submitted, In Review, Action Taken, or Resolved.
Q: Is my personal data safe?
Your data is encrypted. Only your user ID is linked to your report. Contact details are only shared with authorized municipal departments.
